Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a new automated schedule?

expand_more

To set up a new automated schedule, navigate to the 'Schedules' tab, tap the '+' icon, and follow the on-screen prompts to define the dripping frequency, duration, and target moisture levels. Save the schedule to activate it.

What do the nutrition graph readings mean?

expand_more

The nutrition graph displays key metrics like N-P-K levels over time. A rising line indicates nutrient absorption, while a flat or declining line might suggest a need for adjustment in your fertilizer solution.

My system isn't connecting to Wi-Fi. What should I do?

expand_more

First, ensure your Wi-Fi router is on and you are within range. Try rebooting both your SmartDrip controller and your router. If the problem persists, go to 'Settings' > 'Wi-Fi Setup' to re-enter your network credentials.

How do I switch between manual and automated modes?

expand_more

On the main dashboard, you'll find a toggle switch labeled 'Mode'. Simply tap it to switch between 'Auto' for scheduled dripping and 'Manual' for on-demand control.
